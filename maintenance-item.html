Maintenance Services

Maintenance technicians are precious assets to every organization as they perform maintenance tasks to keep your facility in good condition. We provide skilled maintenance workers that will ensure that your building and grounds look clean and maintained at all times.

Service Details

  • Assist With Construction or Remodeling Projects
  • Install and Repair Electric Wiring
  • Inspect Company Equipment
  • Perform Inspection of the Premises
  • Perform Both Major and Minor Repair Work
  • Perform General Maintenance on Sanitary and Mechanical Systems
  • Move Furniture and Equipment
  • Maintain the Overall Condition of the Building and Grounds
  • Emergency and Same Day Services
  • Weekly, Biweekly, and Monthly Appointments
