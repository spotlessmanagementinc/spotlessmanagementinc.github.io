About Oliver

After a highly productive corporate career that took him from the halls of Corporate America to assignments in the Middle East and beyond, Oliver knew he was highly adept at guiding high-performing professionals, organizations, and businesses to become better. Consequently, he turned his attention to entrepreneurship by venturing into his own business. As Managing Partner and Chief Rainmaker at Rainmaker Growth Partners, Oliver has been able to spend considerably more time pursuing his business and personal interests including giving back to the Atlanta community. His business acumen combined with his diverse marketing, sales, and project management experience allow him to easily build strategic relationships both domestically and internationally, as well as online and in-person. This unique skill set enables him to build relationships faster, shorten sales cycles, and increase profits for clients.

As a speaker, trainer, and coach, Mr. Yarbrough offers fresh insights and encourages his audiences and clients to take action with a captivating, entertaining, and down-to-earth style. He shares with them his hands-on experience building and managing professional relationships for some of the largest companies in the world, including Lucent Technologies, Staples, and Sprint, along with his own successful business ventures.

On a personal note, Oliver has a great passion for global cultural immersion. He loves to travel and learn about customs from locals that he meets. He has been fortunate to live, work, and explore such fascinating places as South Africa, Southeast Asia, Saudi Arabia, and Canada.
